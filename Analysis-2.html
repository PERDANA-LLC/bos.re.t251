Executive Summary: The "Execution Gap"

The Context (2025)

The housing market is "frozen" due to rate lock-in. The NAR Settlement has forced Buyer Agents to sign representation agreements *before* touring homes.

The Pain: Agents are terrified. They must justify commissions immediately. They are losing 46% of leads to missed calls and burning out trying to be content creators.

The Arbitrage Opportunity

Zillow Premier Agents pay $1,000+/month for leads they can't convert.

Your Business Model: Sell them the System (Service) to convert those leads, fulfilled by Labor (Fiverr) at 1/10th the cost.
