3. The Supply Chain

Establishing a reliable fulfillment line on Fiverr using the "McKinsey Standard" vetting protocol.

Search Protocol

Don't search generic terms. Use specific 2025 keywords: "Real Estate Reels Premiere Pro" or "GoHighLevel AI Snapshot". Look for "Rising Talent" to get lower rates with high hunger.

Rights & Teams

Critical: Purchase "Commercial Use" rights (check Fiverr TOS). Use the "Fiverr Team Account" feature to manage multiple freelancers and create redundancy (backup providers).

The "Test Flight"

Spend $100 to test 5 different sellers on the same task. The winner becomes Primary; the runner-up is Backup. Never rely on a single node in your supply chain.

White Label Portal

Use a tool like SuiteDash or Moxo. The agent uploads files there; you (or your VA) bridge them to Fiverr. The client never sees Fiverr.

5. Risk & Compliance

Navigating the legal minefield of 2025 (RESPA, TCPA, Unlicensed Assistants).

RESPA (Kickbacks)

Do NOT pay agents for referrals if providing settlement services. As a marketing agency, you are safer, but avoid "co-marketing" cost splits unless strict guidelines are followed.

TCPA (AI Agents)

If your AI receptionist handles calls, it MUST announce it is an automated assistant. Consent to contact is required for all leads.

Unlicensed Activity

Your "Drop Service" VAs cannot negotiate terms or explain contracts. Keep tasks strictly Administrative and Marketing (Photos, Scheduling, Data Entry).
